< !-- FAQ -->
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MyOrbisVoice's AI voice agent platform.

What voice models does MyOrbisVoice support?+
MyOrbisVoice currently supports Google Gemini Live as the primary real-time voice engine, with additional TTS providers including ElevenLabs and Google TTS for non-streaming use cases.
How long does it take to deploy a voice agent?+
Most agents are live in under 5 minutes. Our auto-configuration engine generates a baseline from your business description, and the embed snippet is ready immediately after setup.
Can I integrate with my existing CRM and calendar?+
Yes. MyOrbisVoice natively supports Google Calendar, Calendly, HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe, and Twilio. Custom integrations are available via REST API and webhooks.
Is my call data secure and private?+
All sessions are encrypted in transit and at rest. Multi-tenant isolation ensures your data is never shared. You can also bring your own API keys for full data sovereignty.
What does the free plan include?+
The free plan includes one active voice agent, up to 50 calls/month, basic analytics, and one tool integration. No credit card required to get started.
