\# OrbisVoice — Investor Pitch \& Architecture Summary

\## Real-Time AI Voice Infrastructure for Revenue Automation



\---



\## 1. What OrbisVoice Is



OrbisVoice is a real-time AI voice infrastructure platform that allows businesses to deploy intelligent voice agents capable of:



\- holding natural conversations

\- executing real-world actions (booking, messaging, payments)

\- capturing and converting high-intent leads

\- operating 24/7 without human intervention



This is not a chatbot.



This is a \*\*revenue execution system\*\* powered by voice.



\---



\## 2. The Problem



Businesses lose revenue every day due to:



\- missed calls

\- delayed responses

\- unqualified staff handling high-intent leads

\- fragmented systems (CRM, scheduling, messaging)



Voice remains the highest-converting channel, but:



\- it does not scale

\- it is expensive

\- it is inconsistent



\---



\## 3. The Solution



OrbisVoice replaces missed and inefficient human interactions with:



\- AI voice agents that answer instantly

\- real-time understanding of user intent

\- execution of actions through connected tools

\- consistent, trackable, and scalable conversations



The system connects directly to:



\- calendars

\- CRMs

\- messaging systems

\- telephony (Twilio)

\- internal business workflows



\---



\## 4. How It Works (High-Level)



\### Core Flow



User speaks → AI understands → AI acts → Business outcome achieved



\---



\### System Architecture



\#### 1. Voice Gateway (Core Engine)

\- real-time WebSocket server

\- connects to Google Gemini Live

\- streams audio both directions

\- intercepts and executes tool calls



\#### 2. AI Layer (Gemini Live)

\- speech-to-intent understanding

\- response generation

\- tool invocation



\#### 3. Tool Layer

\- calendar booking

\- SMS/email sending

\- outbound calling

\- CRM updates

\- commerce actions



\#### 4. Policy Layer

\- enforces business rules

\- controls cost

\- ensures compliance

\- prevents unsafe actions



\#### 5. Finance Layer

\- tracks usage (tokens, minutes, messages)

\- calculates cost and revenue

\- manages overages and margins



\#### 6. Admin \& Governance Layer

\- role-based permissions

\- approvals for sensitive actions

\- audit logging

\- enterprise controls
