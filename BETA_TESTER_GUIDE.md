# 🎙️ OrbisVoice Beta Tester Guide

Welcome to the OrbisVoice Beta! We're excited to have you help us refine the future of AI voice agents. This guide will walk you through the core features and how to provide feedback.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Access the Platform
*   **Target URL**: [https://myorbisvoice.com](https://myorbisvoice.com)
*   **Browser**: We recommend using the latest version of **Chrome** or **Edge** for the best voice experience.

### 2. Create Your Account
*   Click **"Sign Up"** on the home page.
*   You will start on the **Free Tier**, which allows you to create **1 Active Agent**.

---

## 🛠️ Testing Checklist

### ✅ 1. Create a Voice Agent
- Navigate to the **Dashboard**.
- Click **"Create New Agent"**.
- Give your agent a name and a **System Prompt** (e.g., "You are a helpful customer service assistant for a coffee shop").
- Select a voice from the Gemini voice options.
- **Goal**: Ensure the agent saves correctly and appears in your list.

### ✅ 2. Test Real-time Voice
- Click the **"Test"** or **"Launch Widget"** button next to your agent.
- Grant microphone permissions when prompted.
- Try talking to your agent. Ask it questions related to your system prompt.
- **Goal**: Verify low latency and natural-sounding responses.

### ✅ 3. Monitor Usage & Transcripts
- After your conversation, check the **Analytic/Transcripts** tab.
- **Goal**: Verify that your conversation was recorded accurately.

### ✅ 4. Refer & Earn
- Go to the **Referrals** page.
- Generate your unique referral link.
- **Goal**: Ensure the link is generated and points to the correct signup page.

### ✅ 5. Subscription Upgrade (Test Mode)
- Attempt to "Upgrade" to the Professional or Enterprise tier.
- If prompted for a card, use the **Stripe Test Card**: `4242 4242 4242 4242` (any valid future expiry/CVC).
- **Goal**: Verify that your account limits (Active Agents) increase immediately after the "payment".

## 🐞 Reporting Issues

If you encounter a bug, a "500 Error", or any unexpected behavior, please let us know:
1.  **What happened?** (e.g., "The mic wouldn't start on Safari").
2.  **Steps to reproduce**: How can we see the error ourselves?
3.  **Screenshot/Console Logs**: If possible, press `F12` and send a copy of any red error text.

---

## ⚠️ Known Limitations
- **Mobile Browsers**: Voice gateway is currently optimized for desktop; mobile support is in early alpha.
- **Simultaneous Calls**: Free tier limits you to 1 active session at a time.

Thank you for helping us build **OrbisVoice**!
